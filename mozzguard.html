The Science Behind Mozz Guard

Mosquitoes dont just fly randomlytheyre drawn to specific cues in their environment, and one of the most powerful is UV light. But not just any UV light.


Scientific research has shown that mosquitoes are most attracted to UV light in the 365-395 nanometer (nm) wavelength range. This particular frequency closely mimics natural light sources they rely on for survivallike moonlight and reflections on water. Since mosquitoes instinctively seek water to breed, their attraction to this wavelength is deeply embedded in their biology.


Many traditional bug zappers use random light sources, but the Mozz Guard is specifically engineered with a high-intensity 365 nm UV LEDthe most attractive wavelength for mosquitoes. By emitting this precise light frequency, the zapper ensures that mosquitoes are lured in efficiently. Once drawn in, they are instantly neutralized by the high-voltage electric grid, preventing them from escaping or biting.
